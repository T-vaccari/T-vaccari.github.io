Neural Network for generating italian names

This project aims to build a neural network capable of generating Italian names. The construction of this network is based on the principles and methodologies outlined in the paper A Neural Probabilistic Language Model by Yoshua Bengio et al. This model utilizes neural network-based approaches to capture the probabilistic structure of language, which we adapt to generate plausible Italian names.
